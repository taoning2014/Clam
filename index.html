Here is our idea

We are surrounded by apps through different platforms: mobile phone, wearable devices, smart TV, VR headset, smart speaker. Wouldn’t it be nice to develop one app that can be used across those platforms(and bring in benefits of each of them)?

Please meet CLAM, an app that helps you to stay focused and be calm throughout your whole day.

Here is how it works: Let’s say you already install it on your phone, the app will ask your permission and detects other devices that connect to your phone. Let’s assume you also have the following devices connected to your phone(under the same account) Apple Watch, Amazon Echo, Smart TV. It will be automatically installed on them.

In the morning, it monitors your sleep cycle through the smartwatch and wakes you up in an appropriate time based on the sleeping cycle by playing nice music in the smart speaker.

After you get up, the app on the TV guide you through a short meditation and body stretch to start your day. At the same time, smartwatch monitor your heartbeat and other health data which helps the program adjust their duration and level of the meditation.
